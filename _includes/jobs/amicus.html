• Amicus

    Having grown out of the Yale Entrepreneurial Society after winning the Yale Venture Challenge, Amicus is a well-funded NYC startup that helps nonprofits and universities raise more money, attract more members, and win more votes. It does this by making it fun and easy for a supporter of a nonprofit to reach out to their friends, family, and neighbors on behalf of a cause they care about. A social good for-profit startup, Amicus believes purpose and profit should go hand in hand.
