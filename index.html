About

HackYale is an organization for students to teach other students various topics and tools in software development with an emphasis on real-world applications. We provide free student-run lectures in web development, introductory programming, and design.

In past years, students who participated in HackYale have gone on to intern or work at companies like Google, Microsoft, Facebook, Twitter, Artsy, Brewster, Foursquare, Origami, Palantir, Panorama Education, Redfin, Trip Advisor, UpWorthy, VigetLabs, and more...

To learn more, please sign up for the HackYale email list, or email team@hackyale.com.
